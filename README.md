# Products' Profit Margin: Calculator and Report
Zen Cart Plugins Download: https://www.zen-cart.com/downloads.php?do=file&id=175

Zen Cart Support Thread: https://www.zen-cart.com/showthread.php?45475-Products-Profit-Margin-Calculator-and-Report-for-1-3-5!3

Well, in short, this plugin allows you to add a cost field to your products and track your profit. It's not a perfect contribution, but its' on its way there. A report under your admin's Reports will show you what your profit is.
