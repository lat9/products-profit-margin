What does this mod do?

Well, in short, it allows you to add a cost field to your products and allow you to track your profit. It's not a perfect contribution, but it's on its way there. A report under Admin->Reports will show you what your profit is.

Installation

  • New install
  • Upgrade
  • Un-install

New install

v3.0.0 and later of the plugin is distributed as a Zen Cart encapsulated plugin, making its installation easier. Unzip the plugin's distribution zip-file and then:

  1. Copy the contents of the plugin's zc_install folder (the ProductsProfitMargin folder) to your store's zc_install folder.
  2. Log into your Zen Cart admin, navigate to Modules :: Plugin Manager, click on the line associated with ProductsProfitMargin and then click the "Install" button.

Once installed, you will be able to edit your products' cost and margin and a report (Reports :: Products Profit) will be available for your use.

Upgrade

Upgrading is easy! Unzip the plugin's distribution zip-file, copy the ProductsProfitMargin folder to your site's zc_plugins folder and sign into your Zen Cart admin. Navigate to Modules :: Plugin Manager, click on the line associated with ProductsProfitMargin and then click the "Upgrade" button.

Un-install

To un-install the plugin, navigate to the admin's Modules :: Plugin Manager, click the line associated with ProductsProfitMargin and then click the "Uninstall" button.

Configuration

Once you have uploaded it, you need to set product cost where you define the products price. You will see more boxes that show up. Please note that product price is automatically generated, so you shouldn't put anything in the original Product Price (net/gross) boxes. Now, put in the cost of the product and the markup percent. Gross margin $ and Gross margin % are automatically calculated for you - just tab through them.

Known issues

  • When an item is deleted, it shows up as cost $0.00 and selling price $0.00
  • If an item's cost is updated, it is reflected for ALL sales. Should only be reflected for sales from now on.
